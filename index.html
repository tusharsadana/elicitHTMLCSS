A initiative never taken before. Until now.

Through the lens of SCIT, ACM-MUJ follows the transformation of Manipal University Jaipur to a technically sound and vibrant University.

Glimpse

The biggest question we ask ourselves as a MUJ student is “what makes us awesome?” What defines a student identity, is it an anthem? A club? Is it a logo or icon? How do these elements shape who we are? In the 1960s and 1970s, these questions were answered by an innovative group of Manipal Founders, who used to lead and to unify the students.
